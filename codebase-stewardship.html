Codebase stewardship is everything your project needs to be sustainable, collaborative and trusted.

Our stewards help guarantee quality and marketability of public-purpose open source projects.

Read about how we steward codebases

Ensure code, tests and docs are
up to standard

Our audit process checks every merge request for quality, enabling agile and trustworthy codebases for public organizations and their collaborators.

Enable community to grow, organize
and build amazing things together

We co-develop communities to help them process feedback and contributions, host events, and execute effective governance.

Build the story that gets code
noticed and re-used

We manage and mature products so they shine on their own, build branding and plan communications to ensure adoption by re-users and contributors, and support both development and design.

Provide tools, skills and answers
for your network

We help developers with support, packaging and distribution, training materials and certifications. We help cooperatives, NGOs and for-profit companies sell, implement, and support products.

Take care of operations and legal affairs

We provide operations support such as infrastructure and process management, as well as legal support such as license advice, IP management, and trademark protection.

Operate globally, think locally

We collaborate with local partners to integrate in their context, while our focus on ecosystems—rather than nations or cities—ensures context-specific code or policy is globally reusable.
